ENROLLEASE: Innovating Student Registration and Timetable for Teachers at Kapitangan National High School

In this project, the primary objective is to revolutionize the student registration and timetable management system at Kapitangan National High School with the creation of the comprehensive enrollment system, "EnrollEase: Innovating Student Registration and Timetable for Teachers at Kapitangan National High School." Tailored to accommodate both old and new students in the junior high level, this system streamlines and enhances the enrollment process. Within the framework of EnrollEase, functionalities extend beyond enrollment to include the creation and management of sections, subjects, teachers, and user accounts.

View project

EnrollEase has a primary focus of introducing a technologically advanced and user-friendly solution for optimizing the registration experience and timetable management that would benefit the whole Kapitangan National High School community. To evaluate the quality of EnrollEase system developed for student registration and timetable management at Kapitangan National High School through a website, ISO/IEC 9126 Software Quality Standards were used including Functionality, Reliability, Usability, Efficiency, and Maintainability. Kapitangan National High School is the beneficiary of this website.
